🎯 Team Tactics

Learn how to coordinate your team like the pros. From executes to site retakes, these strategies will help you win rounds with precision and teamwork.

🧨 Execute Strategies

Well-timed executes are key to taking a bombsite. Use utility to block vision, flush enemies out of hiding, and secure entry kills.

  • 💣 Mirage A Take: smokes on stairs, jungle, and CT
  • 💥 Inferno B Rush: molly dark, smoke CT, flash pool
  • 🔥 Nuke Upper Pop: door rush + heaven smoke + vent molly

🛡️ Defensive Setups

Defend sites with strong positioning, crossfires, and utility usage. Don’t peek alone. Communicate rotations and save utility.

  • 📌 Double pit setup on Inferno A
  • 📌 Crossfire from connector and jungle on Mirage
  • 📌 Heaven and site hold on Overpass B

🔄 Rotations & Retakes

Efficient rotations win rounds. Learn how and when to rotate, and how to execute 2vX or 3vX retakes using teamwork and flashbangs.

  • 🔁 Fast rotate on Vertigo B using mid control
  • 🔦 CT retake with flashes and banana molly on Inferno
  • 🚶 Silent rotates with smoke cover on Ancient
